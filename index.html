Vaccination Education at UCLA

Educating Underpriviledged Neighborhoods on the Importance of Vaccines

About Us

We are an outreach organization dedicated to educating elementary through high school students in the Los Angeles community on the benefits of vaccination, in particular the Human Papillomavirus. Our approach targets students as well as parents and teachers, and focuses on discussing misconceptions by an interactive approach to education.we educate students on the importance of vaccination from a scientific and research based perspective.
